Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Grammar Highlighter ("the App") is a desktop application designed to help users analyze documents with grammar highlighting and note-taking features.

1. Data Collection and Storage

The App is a "local-first" application. All your documents, notes, and highlights are stored locally on your device's hard drive.

We (the developers) do not have access to your data, documents, or personal information. We do not operate a central server to store your content.

2. Google Drive Integration

The App offers an optional feature to synchronize your data with your personal Google Drive account.

  • Access Scope: The App requests access only to files and folders created by the App itself (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file). It cannot access your other private files.
  • Usage: This access is used strictly to upload and download a backup of your notes (`grammar-highlighter-data.json`) and any document files you explicitly choose to sync.
  • Data Safety: Your data is transferred directly between your device and Google's servers. It does not pass through any third-party servers owned by us.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses local Natural Language Processing (NLP) libraries. No text content is sent to external servers for analysis.
